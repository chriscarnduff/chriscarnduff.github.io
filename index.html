Portfolio

Aqua Bicylinder

The Aqua Bicylinder is a design solution that provides people in rural Africa with a method to transport large amounts of clean drinking water from a distant source. The device consists of a large removable barrel that attaches to the rear of a bicycle to prevent physical stress on the user. This is superior to the current solution where children and woman are required to walk up to 7 km with 25-liter jerry cans causing physical injury and other complications. The design also decreases the amount of time required to collect water allowing the woman and children who typically hold this responsibility to attend school or work to provide food for their family.

Snaptrax

Snaptrax was created at the NHacks hackathon in London Ontario. The site takes an image URL and takes advantage of an artificial intelligence image processing to analyze what is in the image to match it with a song. The idea came from the many memories that I can associate with specific songs. I wanted a way to match my photos with a new song. Other applications that our team had discussed if the project were to continue would include matching music with videos or presentations for commercial uses. There are currently a few features that are in the process of being added. These include:

  • A feed of recent photo's that have been snaptracked by users
  • A means to upload and store photos from users
  • A more complex and accurate matching process
